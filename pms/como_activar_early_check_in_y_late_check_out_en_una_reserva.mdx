---
title: "¿Cómo activar early check in y late check out en una reserva?"
description: "En esta guía explicaremos cómo indicar que un huésped realiza early check in o late check out para una habitación."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accede a la reserva correspondiente">
    Ingresa a la reserva que necesitas modificar.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/5304717390942074030448600db1fad8f488ae27e2.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T173210Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=53e1badcbaa55ff6ef2aebd91fa2f5905cc99fb5791052c1e40c5e2e65b031cf" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Selecciona la solapa de Habitaciones y Huéspedes">
    A continuación, haz clic en la solapa **Habitaciones y Huéspedes**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/28291828144fb4049abf6de4f6057586302131edb5e.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T173419Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=9cd76ca6aa657ccaf643ddfdf637bec090026294d892f0ce741a10df8daa2094" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edita la habitación">
    En la columna de acciones, selecciona **Acciones** y luego haz clic en **Editar habitación**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/5304718263599081c7d6217618426e9c7809813ef0.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T173701Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=ce40e13a039fa7116328a7a75674138d26510b1545e705529b2c80af8378dc5e" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/353647885497acdb1d78849f2ec19f07280c28d107.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T173707Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=70c0c6f23b6bf08b23fb5a52fa682af2cb43f63490bb327b682beb05de1accd8" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activa Early Check In o Late Check Out">
    Haz clic en el botón del campo **early check in (ECI)** o **late check out (LCO)** hasta que se ponga en color verde.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/24755353728e40d392b51714c89d2828c1bad804a09.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T173912Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=7ab705407a1a67ac9245e5ede0076f3a10753b330ae453fd3930feda4e1caac3" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/194506351601ed7611ed002b00f98b7611ca4059dae.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T173920Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=41bf4421c12a559016a41a7f77ed6a633e7f3fe37d238b0acb40b295c8d038f2" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verifica y ajusta la tarifa">
    Se agregará automáticamente un campo indicando la tarifa correspondiente al early check in o late check out, calculada según el porcentaje configurado en el Establecimiento. Puedes modificar este valor manualmente si es necesario.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://files-public-web.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/1978/company/library/user/15914156679d930e88efe096b007ee1500f5248639c.png?X-Amz-Content-Sha256=UNSIGNED-PAYLOAD&X-Amz-Algorithm=AWS4-HMAC-SHA256&X-Amz-Credential=AKIAQI4Y7PQJFLSSFFFR%2F20260112%2Fus-west-2%2Fs3%2Faws4_request&X-Amz-Date=20260112T174031Z&X-Amz-SignedHeaders=host&X-Amz-Expires=1200&X-Amz-Signature=70b4e0c0b0712204cddfd71cd0420ad156a8fbf96d4a965ed724a7ec3450e7c3" alt="Imagen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Guarda los cambios">
    Finalmente, haz clic en "Guardar Cambios" para aplicar las modificaciones.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://cdn2.hubspot.net/hubfs/7398646/image-308.png" alt="Guardar cambios" />
    </Frame>

    <Check>
      Has activado correctamente el early check in o late check out para la reserva.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
